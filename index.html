RTI

MTNL respects the Right to Information Act 2005 and is committed to providing transparent access to information for all citizens.

Citizens can request relevant information pertaining to MTNL's operations, policies, and services under this act.

How to File an RTI Application

  • Applications can be submitted on plain paper
  • Can be submitted in writing, via fax, or through internet
  • Applications accepted in English, Hindi, or local language
  • Fees are as defined by Government of India (GOI)
